About Me

Michael Cumings is a new Full Stack Web Developer (having completed the University of Minnesota Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp program) living in Fargo, ND with his two children.

He is a dedicated researcher, has experience making corporate training materials, and has an academic background in Church History and Philosophy. He has an interest in projects related to tabletop games as well as APIs for Google services, and lately ML Kit.

He has experience coding HTML, CSS, JavaScript and associated frameworks (particularly React), and SQL and NoSQL databases. He is determined to continue exploring new technologies to better meet any professional challenge.

Substantial projects include What's In There?, an app that reads barcode labels on containers to keep track of their contents using Google Photos; Peek, a rudimentary social network platform linking photos with locations and times; and Baseline, a web application for evaluating professional self-presentation. Beta versions of these will be posted in the Portfolio section as they become available. For now, the Portfolio section shows some coding exercises I have completed.
